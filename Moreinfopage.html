About Hauser Bears

Hauser Bears is a UK based charity which works to conserve bears around the globe through education and research. Their main focus is to change attitudes towards bear bile farming in South East Asia, the poaching for body parts and captive bears in the entertainment industry such as dancing bears.

How You Can Help

You can help Hauser Bears protect bears worldwide by making a donation on their website or through fundraising. For the adventurous, you can participate in their polar bear expedition in the Arctic.
